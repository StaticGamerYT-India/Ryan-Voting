About This Voting System

This voting system was created for Ryan International School Dumas Council Elections 2025-2026.

Features:

  • Class-wise voting system
  • Real-time results tracking
  • Vote prevention (one vote per class)
  • Export functionality for results
  • Settings management
  • Responsive design

How to Use:

  1. Select your class from the dropdown
  2. Choose your preferred candidate
  3. Click the Vote button
  4. View results in real-time
